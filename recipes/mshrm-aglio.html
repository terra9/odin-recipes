Mushroom Aglio Olio Recipe



Description

This is an italian dish that is perfect for vegans. This dish uses mushroom instead of meat, but you can still substitute it with meat.

Ingredients

  • 200ml of pasta.
  • 100gr of white mushroom.
  • olive oil.
  • garlic.
  • dried chilli.
  • seasalt.
  • basil.
  • 500ml of water.

Steps

  1. cut garlic into thin slices.
  2. cut the white mushroom vertically.
  3. put water into the pot and add generous amount of salt then turn on the stove.
  4. put the pasta into the pot and turn off the heat if it cooked.
  5. while waiting for the pasta to cook, put olive oil to the saute pan and turn on the stove to medium heat.
  6. put the garlic into the pan and stir fry it unitl it started to have golden color.
  7. put the mushroom into the pan and stir fry it until it started to brown.
  8. add salt and dried chilli into the pan and stir fry it for 4 minutes.
  9. add generous amount of pasta water into the saute pan and let it simmer until it has small amount of water in it then turn the heat off.
  10. take out the pasta from the pot and put it into the pan.
  11. stir the pan until the pasta is covered in sauce.
  12. put it all into a plate and add a handful of basil.
